Introduction

The Capacity Building Programme in Artificial Intelligence is designed to equip educators and faculty members with foundational AI knowledge, practical applications, and pedagogical strategies for effective integration into teaching, research, and administration.

About Artificial Intelligence

Artificial Intelligence (AI) refers to the development of systems that can perform tasks typically requiring human-like intelligence, such as problem-solving, decision-making, and pattern recognition.

AI enhances classroom engagement, personalizes learning, and streamlines administrative processes. For educators, it's essential to understand AI tools and integrate them into learning experiences that support critical thinking and accessibility.

Programme Objectives

  • Understand AI concepts and tools relevant to education.
  • Use AI-driven teaching strategies.
  • Apply AI in research and academic tasks.
  • Gain hands-on experience with AI technologies.
  • Build a network of AI-enabled educators.

Target Audience

This programme is designed for college faculty across disciplines who wish to improve their AI literacy and explore the transformative role of AI in education and research.

Structure & MEME Approach

The FDP is modular with 5 modules across 5 days within a month. The Multi Entry Multi Exit (MEME) system offers flexibility to accommodate busy faculty schedules, allowing them to complete the program at their own pace.

Certification is awarded upon completion of all five modules.
